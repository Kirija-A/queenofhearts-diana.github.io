Princess of Wales

Diana, princess of Wales, original name Diana Frances Spencer, (born July 1, 1961, Sandringham, Norfolk, England, died August 31, 1997, Paris, France). She was a member of the British royal family. She was the first wife of Charles, Prince of Wales. Her first child, Prince William Arthur Philip Louis of Wales, was born on June 21, 1982, and her second child, Prince Henry (“Harry”) Charles Albert David, on September 15, 1984.
